## App Description

### Short Description (30 chars)
Track prices. Save money.

### Full Description
Stop impulse buying with smart price tracking.

**How it works:**
1. Add any product from your browser
2. Set a cooldown period (7, 14, or 30 days)
3. We track the price—but won't notify you until the cooldown ends
4. Make rational decisions, not emotional ones

**Features:**
• Universal price tracking - works on Amazon, Walmart, Target, Best Buy, and thousands more
• Price drop alerts after your cooldown period
• Price history charts
• Chrome extension for easy tracking
• Share your savings with friends

**Pricing:**
• Free: Track 1 product
• Unlock All ($4.99): Track 10 products
• Premium ($2.99/mo): Unlimited tracking + priority alerts

Stop the scroll. Beat the algorithm. Keep your money.

# Privacy Policy for Stop Impulsing

**Last Updated:** February 6, 2026

## Overview

Stop Impulsing ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application and Chrome browser extension.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address**: Used for account creation and authentication via Supabase Auth.
- **Password**: Securely hashed and stored; we never have access to your plain-text password.

### Product Tracking Data
- **Product URLs**: URLs you choose to track for price monitoring.
- **Price history**: Historical price data we collect when checking your tracked products.
- **Product metadata**: Title, images, and store information extracted from product pages.

### Device Information
- **Push notification tokens**: Used to send you price drop alerts (iOS only).
- **Device identifiers**: Anonymous identifiers for crash reporting and analytics.

### Usage Data
- **App interactions**: Anonymous usage patterns to improve the app experience.
- **Feature usage**: Which features you use most frequently.

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Provide Core Services**: Track product prices and notify you of price changes.
2. **Personalization**: Remember your preferences and cooldown settings.
3. **Communication**: Send price alerts and important service updates.
4. **Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve the app.
5. **Support**: Respond to your support requests.

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using **Supabase** (PostgreSQL database with row-level security).
- Data transmission is encrypted using **TLS 1.3**.
- Authentication uses industry-standard **JWT tokens**.
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## Data Retention

- **Account data**: Retained while your account is active.
- **Price history**: Retained for 12 months, then automatically deleted.
- **Deleted accounts**: All associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data.
- **Delete**: Delete your account and all associated data.
- **Export**: Export your tracked products and price history.
- **Opt-out**: Disable push notifications at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **privacy@stopimpulsing.app**

## Children's Privacy

Stop Impulsing is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Chrome Extension Specific

The Stop Impulsing Chrome Extension:
- **Only activates** on e-commerce product pages you visit.
- **Does not track** your browsing history.
- **Does not inject ads** or modify page content beyond the "Track Price" button.
- **Requires permissions** only for the specific sites where you choose to track products.
